Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?
We offer free delivery within Harare CBD. For other locations, shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on your address.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns on unused items in their original condition within 14 days of purchase. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.
Do you offer a warranty on your products?
Yes, all our products come with a 1-year warranty covering craftsmanship and material defects.
How do I care for my leather product?
We recommend using a quality leather conditioner periodically. Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight and water. For specific care instructions, please refer to the product page.
